Our story

Started in Mandi Bahauddin, built for the whole country.

Ansari Tours & Rent a Car started with a simple idea: renting a car or booking a tour in Pakistan shouldn't mean guessing about the vehicle's condition or the driver's route knowledge. We vet every vehicle before it goes out, and every driver knows the roads they're assigned — whether that's a city transfer or a pass through the Karakoram.

Today we run a mixed fleet of sedans, SUVs, 7-seaters and coasters, alongside four signature tour routes and fully custom itineraries for families, groups and companies.

What we stand for

Vehicle Condition

Driver Trust
